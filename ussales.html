Sales Data Analysis

PROBLEM

The project aimed to explore various metrics from a sales dataset using SQL. After performing essential data cleaning, extensive SQL queries were executed to derive meaningful insights. By leveraging window functions, Common Table Expressions (CTEs), GROUP BY clauses, subqueries, and joins, the following metrics were extracted:


  • Total sales.
  • Monthly sales.
  • Cities with the highest sales and their contribution to total sales.
  • Best-selling product and its contribution to overall sales.
  • Sales performance of the best-selling product across all demographics.
  • Relationships between products.
  • Top markets for individual products.
  • First product purchased by consumers.
  • New customers added each month.
